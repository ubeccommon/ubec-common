The Eight Services

The UBEC Ecosystem

Eight interconnected services — one philosophical foundation. Each service is a whole unto itself and a part of the larger commons.

🔗
Economic Engine
UBEC Protocol

The DAO protocol network running live on Stellar. Houses the four-element token economy (Air, Water, Earth, Fire) and governance infrastructure for 502 active stewards.

bioregional.ubec.network 📡
Citizen Science Network
UBEC Living Labs

Register as an environmental steward, connect a senseBox IoT sensor, and contribute phenomenological observations to the bioregional commons. Earn UBECrc tokens for each contribution.

living-labs.ubec.network 🗺️
Bioregional Intelligence
UBEC Maps

Bioregional GIS mapping built on Mapbender with HydroSHEDS watershed data. Visualises natural boundaries, community assets, and token economic flows across the bioregion.

mapservice.ubec.network ⚙️
Data & API Services
UBEC Hub

The central REST API and IoT integration layer. All data flows route through the Hub — including the 13 Questions to the Soil module, senseBox coordination, and Gaia-X compliant data services.

iot.ubec.network 🌱
Flagship Living Laboratory
Erdpuls by UBEC

A Center for Sustainability Literacy in Müllrose, Brandenburg — 3,000m² of living laboratory. Offers workshops, a Community Seed Bank, and a Collective Threshold funding model for regenerative offerings.

erdpuls.ubec.network 📚

The Four-Element Token Economy

A Living Economic System

Four tokens — four elements — one generative sequence from universal access to collective transformation. Live on the Stellar network.

Air · Universal Access
UBEC

The primary circulation token. Gateway to the protocol network — open to all stewards entering the commons.

Water · Reciprocity
UBECrc

Earned through phenomenological observations and citizen science contributions. Data flows generate reciprocity value.

Earth · Stability
UBECgpi

The stable store and documentation anchor. Grounds the protocol in place — the soil beneath the digital commons.

Fire · Transformation
UBECtt

Activates governance and collective transformation. The element of action — when commons decisions must become reality.

Intellectual Foundations

Built on Five Frameworks

These are not decorative references — they are operational design constraints. Every feature, data structure, and API decision is legible through at least one of these lenses.

🤝
Ubuntu Philosophy
"I am because we are"

The foundational ethics of the entire ecosystem. Personhood is constituted through relationship — collective governance over individual extraction.

🏛️
Christopher Alexander
Pattern Language & Living Structure

The OER repository is a genuine Pattern Language of Place. The platform evolves through unfolding wholeness — not imposed structure.

🗺️
Edward T. Hall
Proxemics & The Hidden Dimension

Data visibility follows proxemic zones — intimate, personal, social, public. Space communicates. The map is a proxemic diagram.

🌿
Goethean Science
Phenomenological Observation

The 13 Questions to the Soil embody Goethe's delicate empiricism. Stewards observe phenomena first, interpret second. Quality of attention generates value.

🔮
Buckminster Fuller
Synergetics & Ephemeralization

The whole is more than the sum of parts. Doing more with less. The microservices architecture embodies the geodesic principle — each node bears its own load.

Net-positive by design

Not sustainable — regenerative. The Collective Threshold funding model, UBECrc observation rewards, and ecosystem health metrics are net-positive by architecture.
