Why Join the Living Labs Network?

Six principles that guide how we observe, record, and regenerate together.

👁️

Phenomenological Observation

We observe phenomena as they present themselves — honouring both human perception and technological sensing in equal measure.

🔗

Blockchain-Recorded Science

Every observation is recorded on the Stellar blockchain, creating an immutable, transparent record of place-based knowledge.

🤝

Reciprocal Economy

Stewards receive UBECrc tokens for their observations — building a regenerative data economy where contribution is rewarded.

🎓

Learning by Doing

Students and citizen scientists develop genuine scientific literacy through real environmental monitoring — not simulations.

🌎

Open Science

All data is transparent and accessible, contributing to a shared understanding of bioregional patterns and living systems.

♻️

Regenerative by Design

Systems that give back more than they take — honouring the living processes of soil, water, air, and community.
